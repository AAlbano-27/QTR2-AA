FACILITIES

The campus boasts state-of-the-art classrooms, fully furnished computer and science labs, and a well-stocked library to promote academic excellence. Along with promoting physical fitness and a balanced lifestyle, the school takes pride in its sports facilities, which include courts and playing fields. PSHS-CRC also gives students access to state-of-the-art equipment, which promotes creativity and research. Overall, these facilities help PSHS-CRC provide a comprehensive educational experience by giving students what they need to succeed both in school and in life.
