CLUBS

Through a wide variety of clubs, Philippine Science High School - CARAGA offers a lively and interesting extracurricular environment. These groups support a wide range of interests, including sports, the arts, community service, and academic endeavors. PSHS-CRC offers its students the chance to get involved in clubs that support their overall development and coincide with their interests. Whether they are a sports team, a science or math club, an art or music society, or a community service organization, these clubs are vital to the student body's development as a sense of identity, skill improvement, and personal growth. Offering a wide variety of clubs demonstrates the school's commitment to developing well-rounded people with a wide range of skills and interests.

SIGALAB


Sigalab is a renowned performing arts club that has mastered the art of dance. Their breathtaking performances, from ballet to hip-hop, leave audiences in awe. They captivate with their versatility, dedication, and commitment to excellence. Sigalab's choreography tells stories and evokes emotions, leaving a lasting impression. Don't miss the opportunity to witness their talent, passion, and artistry. Sigalab is more than a dance club; they symbolize dedication, creativity, and the power of expression through dance. Let's applaud Sigalab, the force to be reckoned with in the world of dance.


SILAKBO


Silakbo, the Glee Club, is a vibrant community of talented individuals who share a deep love for music. With their incredible musical prowess, Silakbo transforms ordinary events into extraordinary experiences, captivating audiences with their versatile performances. As a close-knit family, Silakbo supports and uplifts its members, fostering an environment where talents flourish and dreams come true. If you believe in the power of music to touch hearts and bring people together, Silakbo welcomes you to join their community and create beautiful music together.


DIBUHO


Calling all talented artists! The Dibuho, also known as the Art Club, is the perfect place for you to explore your artistic abilities and create stunning works of art. Join this vibrant community of artists at Pisay and unlock various techniques to enhance your drawing skills. Showcase your talent at the annual Art Exhibition and connect with like-minded individuals who share your passion for art. Join the Dibuho today and unleash your creativity! "Every artist was first an amateur." - Ralph Waldo Emerson.


HOME MAKERS


At Pisay (Philippine Science High School), Home Economics clubs create a nurturing environment where students can express themselves creatively while mastering the art of cooking. These clubs go beyond traditional classroom experiences, offering joy, fun, and a chance to explore the beauty of everyday life. Students learn not only how to cook but also how to be expressive, infusing their own style and personality into their culinary creations. Home Economics at Pisay imparts valuable life skills and fosters camaraderie among students. It's an enriching experience where joy, beauty, and cooking intertwine to create lasting memories and a love for self-expression.

DEBATE CIRCLE

The club is known for its inclusive community and exciting events like the Pisay Intrams. Join today and unleash your athletic prowess! RED CROSS YOUTH


Pisay, or the Philippine Science High School, is renowned for its academic excellence and also houses a Red Cross Club. This club teaches students valuable skills like wound care and provides aid during illnesses. It plays a crucial role in ensuring student safety during school events, with trained members always ready to assist. The Red Cross Club fosters empathy and compassion, instilling values of kindness and resilience. It is a shining example of a school community promoting safety, well-being, and compassion, while equipping students with essential first aid skills. Pisay's Red Cross Club creates an environment where every student feels safe and cared for, fostering growth and learning.
